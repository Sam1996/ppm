About Us

QUALITY IS OUR DUTY, OUR MOTTO IS SAFETY

We are proud to introduce that we are the leading Packers and Movers Transport Contractors in Bangalore, providing reliable packing and transportation services in Balgalore since 1969. We offer expert Packing and Moving sevices, Relocation services which includes household goods shifting, loading-unloading services, Relocation of office goods, car moving and transportation, warehousing and goods insurance service etc. to clients. We see ourselves as committed professional, harmoniously employing technology, innovation and the human touch to achieve customer satisfaction and goodwill-the corner-stones of our success and the focus of all our efforts.

Our Mission is to continue offering our best services to our clients.

Plan Packers and Movers is a prominent name in the Packing and Transportation industry not only in the field of Household goods packing and moving but also Commercial/ Corporate goods shifting, Industrial packing, Cargo Transportation etc.We render swift, steady and specialized logistics solutions for any kind of household goods, corporate goods or industrial goods transportation.Our quality services have earned us clients not only from Bangalore but from all over India. Our satisfied clients include Reserve Bank of India, all Nationalised and Multi National Banks, Multi National companies, Software companies, major industrial houses etc.
